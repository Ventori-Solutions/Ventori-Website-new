Frequently Asked Questions

Who can benefit from your training programs?

Our programs are designed for startups, SMEs and enterprises globally. Whether you’re a non‑technical executive or an experienced developer, you’ll gain practical skills and insights.

How are your courses delivered?

We offer live online courses, on‑site training and self‑paced modules to suit different learning styles and schedules.

Do you provide certification?

Yes. Each course level includes optional exams. Successful candidates receive a certificate recognising their achievement.

What is your implementation approach?

We follow an agile methodology, starting with discovery, prototyping, development and deployment. We ensure transparent communication throughout.

What industries do you specialise in?

We work across industries including finance, healthcare, manufacturing and consumer goods. Our expertise is adaptable to any domain.

What tools and technologies do you use?

We leverage modern open‑source tools such as n8n, Qdrant, Ollama and LangChain alongside enterprise‑grade platforms to build scalable, secure solutions tailored to your needs.

Do you offer custom workshops?

Absolutely! We design bespoke workshops around your data, processes and goals so your team can immediately apply what they learn to real projects.

How long does it take to implement a solution?

Implementation timelines vary depending on scope. A typical project—from discovery to deployment—can take anywhere from a few weeks to a few months. We’ll provide a detailed roadmap during your consultation.

Can you help us define an AI strategy?

Yes. Our consultants work with your leadership team to develop an AI adoption roadmap aligned with your business objectives, helping you prioritise use cases and build internal capability.

What support is available after training?

We offer ongoing mentoring, Q&A sessions and technical support to ensure your teams continue to grow and your solutions stay up‑to‑date.
